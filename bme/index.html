ABOUT

ASKLEPIUS 2K24

ASKLEPIUS 2K24, a premier national-level symposium by the Biomedical Engineering Department at Alpha College of Engineering, where cutting-edge innovations, expert discussions, and groundbreaking research converge to shape the future of healthcare.

DEPARTMENT OF BME

The Department of Biomedical Engineering started in the year 2006. The department works towards helping students gain comprehensive knowledge of the industrial requirements.The department is associated with BMESI (Bio-Medical Engineering Society of India) the Professional body for BME.
